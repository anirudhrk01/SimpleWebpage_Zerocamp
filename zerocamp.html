Welcome to ZeroCamp

Empowering your tech journey, ZeroCamp is here to provide you with free and comprehensive education in the world of coding and tech. Join us in a community-driven learning experience where curiosity thrives, mentorship flourishes, and opportunities abound.

About ZeroCamp

ZeroCamp is a fully free and open, online coding bootcamp.

ZeroCamp aims to help beginners launch their careers in the tech industry (no prior tech or coding experience needed). The boot camp provides a structured curriculum with weekly topics for learning, allowing students to choose their own resources/use recommended learning resources suggested by mentors. Each week, mentors conduct reviews and mock interviews to test students' understanding and progress. Students receive guidance to develop personal projects and build a portfolio as part of the curriculum.

ZeroCamp is a free and volunteer-run program dedicated to providing students with an enriching learning experience in the tech field. Here are some highlights of what we offer:

  • Free education: ZeroCamp is entirely free and doesn't require anything in return from students.
  • Mentorship: Weekly calls with mentors to assess progress and address learning gaps.
  • Job readiness preparation: Learn essential skills for tech jobs, including resume writing and interview techniques.
  • Community: Join a passionate community focused on learning to code and tech.
  • Open startup philosophy: ZeroCamp operates following the Open startup philosophy, making the program and its operations open for adaptation and replication.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ZeroCamp really free?

Yes, ZeroCamp is entirely free for all participants. We believe in providing accessible education for everyone.

How can I apply to ZeroCamp?

The application process will be detailed on our website once registrations open. Stay tuned!

What kind of mentorship can I expect?

Students enrolled in ZeroCamp will have weekly calls with experienced mentors who will assess progress and provide guidance.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

Yes, participants who successfully complete the program will receive a certificate of completion.

What programming languages or skills will be taught?

ZeroCamp covers a wide range of tech-related topics, including but not limited to HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and more.

Can I access ZeroCamp materials after the program ends?

Absolutely! All learning materials will be accessible even after the program concludes.
